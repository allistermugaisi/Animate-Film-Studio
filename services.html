Our Services

Courses
We offer various kinds of courses from online courses to summer courses and intensive courses.
Podcast
Streaming applications and podcasting services provide a convenient and integrated way to manage a personal consumption queue across many podcast sources and playback devices.
Animation
In animate studio, animation provides a method of photographing successive drawings, models, or even puppets, to create an illusion of movement in a sequence.
Modelling & Texture
Students studying Modeling and Texturing are guided through the process of 3D asset creation for films. To develop a strong understanding of form, texture, and detail, students are trained in the fundamentals of anatomy, sculpture, painting, and design.
Film
Film and it's innovations sometimes has to catch up to society but sometimes it leads society too. They help the economy grow, inspire individuals, and expand our basic knowledge of the world around us.
Camera & lights
Photographic lighting is the illumination of scenes to be photographed. Lighting and exposure are used to create effects such as low-key and high-key.
